Team Builder

Build Your Own Team

Ready to lead? Follow these steps to create and assemble your dream hackathon squad.

🎯
Step 1

Choose Your Hackathon

Browse the calendar above and lock in a hackathon. Check the theme, tech stack, team size, and deadline before you start recruiting.

  • Match the theme to your strongest skill
  • Check prize eligibility (student / open)
  • Note the submission deadline carefully
📋
Step 2

Define Your Roles

Before recruiting, map out exactly what skills your project needs — not just "developers". Clear roles attract the right people.

  • Frontend / Backend / Full-Stack
  • ML Engineer or Data Scientist
  • UI/UX Designer (don't skip this!)
  • DevOps / Presenter / Project Lead
🤝
Step 3

Recruit Teammates

Use the Team Finder above to connect with members whose skills complement yours. Send a clear message with your project idea and what role you need filled.

  • Be specific about the hackathon and role
  • Share a one-line project idea
  • Aim for 2–4 members max for agility
⚙️
Step 4

Set Up Your Workspace

Before the hack starts, agree on your tools and workflow so you don't waste precious hours during the event.

  • Create a shared GitHub org / repo
  • Set up a Discord or Slack channel
  • Choose a project management tool (Notion / Trello)
  • Agree on a tech stack and architecture
🚀
Step 5

Plan Your 48 Hours

A winning team plans before they code. Divide the hackathon into phases and assign responsibilities upfront.

  • Hours 0–4: Ideate, wireframe, plan
  • Hours 4–36: Build core features only
  • Hours 36–44: Polish UI and fix bugs
  • Hours 44–48: Prepare demo & deck
🏆
Step 6

Present & Win

A great product with a poor demo won't win. Spend real time rehearsing your pitch — judges respond to clarity and confidence.

  • Lead with the problem, not the tech
  • Live demo > slides whenever possible
  • Keep it to 2–3 minutes max
  • Anticipate judge questions in advance

Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to HackHub. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our platform.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including account details (name, email, password), profile information (skills, location, bio), communication data, and usage data about how you interact with our platform.

Third-Party Authentication

HackHub offers sign-in through Google and GitHub. When you use these services we receive only the information you authorize (name, email, profile picture). We never store your third-party passwords.

How We Use Your Information

We use your data to create and manage your account, match you with relevant hackathons, facilitate team connections, send transactional emails, analyze usage patterns to improve our services, and comply with legal obligations.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share it with trusted service providers under confidentiality agreements, or when required by law.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data. Contact us at hello@hackhub.io to exercise these rights.

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including SSL/TLS encryption and access controls. No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.
